Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for your programs?

You can register for our programs through our Events page or by contacting us directly. We'll provide you with all the necessary information and registration forms.

Do you offer programs for different age groups?

Yes! Our programs are designed for young people aged 13-30, with age-appropriate content and activities for different developmental stages.

How can my school partner with YADA-EXPERIENCE?

We'd love to partner with your school! Contact us to discuss how we can bring our purpose discovery programs to your students.

Are your programs free?

Many of our programs are offered at no cost to participants, thanks to our donors and partners. Some specialized workshops may have a small fee to cover materials.
